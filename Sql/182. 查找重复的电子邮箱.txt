编写一个 SQL 查询，查找 Person 表中所有重复的电子邮箱。

示例：

+----+---------+
| Id | Email   |
+----+---------+
| 1  | a@b.com |
| 2  | c@d.com |
| 3  | a@b.com |
+----+---------+
根据以上输入，你的查询应返回以下结果：

+---------+
| Email   |
+---------+
| a@b.com |
+---------+

select Email from Person group by Email having count(Email)>1
//group by 是分组
//having count()筛选数量
